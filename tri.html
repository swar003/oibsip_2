A. P. J. Abdul Kalam
(1931-2015)



"Don't take rest after your first victory because if you fail in the second, more lips are waiting to say that your first victory was just luck."

BIOGRAPHY

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and passed away on July 27, 2015. Dr. Kalam played a crucial role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.
